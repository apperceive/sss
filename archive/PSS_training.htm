Parent Success Skills

Training

The Parent Success Skills program is designed to supplement the Student Success Skills and the Ready for Success programs.  The program developers, Dr. Paul Peluso and Dr. Greg Brigman can provide training for school districts for the PSS program. 

Although the detailed PSS manual is designed to be used without training, we have found that training makes it easier for counselors and teachers to implement the program with a high degree of confidence right away.  We can also work with school districts to develop a train the trainer model so that future trainings can be led by district staff.

Many districts use Title I: Instructional strategies and staff development, Safe and Drug Free Schools, SAI, and Local School Improvement funds to finance PSS training. 

We also consult with school districts on how to easily collect accountability data that shows that the SSS program makes a difference in student academic performance. If your district is interested in training please contact us at SSSAEC@aol.com.
